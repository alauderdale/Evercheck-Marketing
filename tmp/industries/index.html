Mobile Carriers

Mobile carriers count on VIAM as a trusted partner for developing needed infrastructure and providing network-based services in remote locations. We have successfully taken on the toughest terrain that mobile carriers face – from steep mountainous areas to the massive Western plains with limited or no communications infrastructure to work with.

Case Study

VIAM was engaged by a wireless carrier to provide a wireless backhaul solution in Durango, Colorado between their remote cell site and switching infrastructure using fiber optic-based transport. It was a hard problem to solve because it required extending fiber optic cable across government land to the top of a local mountain.

By working with numerous local resources and navigating rights of way issues, we were able to successfully complete the project enabling the carrier to execute its 4G strategy in the region.

Expanding Networks

National and regional carriers are constantly extending their networks into new areas, but sometimes they lack the local knowledge to pull everything together. When they need assistance developing a solution, we’re able to jump in to provide:

  • Network-based transport service such as backhaul
  • Asset development including new fiber, microwave or tower solutions
  • Bigger communications pipes between cell towers and centralized switches

With our assistance, mobile carriers are able to meet a wide range of objectives, including:

  • Improving coverage areas without outrageous operating costs
  • Employing the latest (4G or 5G) technology to compete with other carriers
