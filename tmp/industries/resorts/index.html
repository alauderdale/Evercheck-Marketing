Resorts and parks

People visit resorts, state parks and national parks for the unparalleled beauty and experiences they offer. And when they arrive, they expect a communications network that will provide the outstanding coverage, capacity and reliability they’re used to back home.

Unfortunately, delivering such a connected experience can prove extremely difficult due to limited infrastructure, the rugged terrain and the remoteness of the location. But if visitors don’t get the coverage they expect, they may choose to shorten their stay or to opt for a different location on their next trip.

Case Study

Taos Ski Valley and the Village of Taos Ski Valley engaged VIAM to improve existing wireless communications and provide a strategy for a long-term solution that could provide reliable mobile wireless capacity and coverage. As a remote and pristine site, the residents, visitors and employees have long been underserved by both wireline and wireless communications.

VIAM worked closely with the existing wireless carriers and local fixed wireless and wireline providers to bring additional backhaul capacity into the carrier cell sites to increase capacity and reduce call blocking

Enriching The Outdoor Experience

As a western company, VIAM embraces the difficulties that come with the rugged west. In fact, our love for the great outdoors and our passion for world-class telecommunications drives everything we do. It’s why we’re able to design, implement and operate complex wireless solutions in some of the most challenging locations around – to deliver the communications services that keep them here longer and keep them coming back.

While it’s true that the costs of setting up networks in remote or mountainous areas can be high, it’s often quite easy to make the business case to do so in places like ski areas or national parks. After all, today’s consumers demand to be able to surf the Internet from their campsite or make a business call from the chair lift.
