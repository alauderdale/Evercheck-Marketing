VIAM Partnership

The scope and scale of developing telecommunications solutions is complex. Often, this requires partnerships with both private and public sector entities. VIAM has successfully partnered with rural wireline communications providers to deliver mobile cell site backhaul solutions in numerous rural locations. VIAM also partners with electric utilities to design and deploy fiber optic cabling in rural metropolitan communities.

Get In Touch

VIAM offers extensive experience in developing contractual relationships with mobile cellular carriers and a highly successful track record working with other partners. If you are a rural wireline service provider wanting to leverage your network infrastructure, please contact us.
